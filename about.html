ABOUT



EDUCATION

Adam grew up in Bealeton, Virginia.
He began his high school career at Liberty High School in 2008.
In 2012, he graduated from Liberty High School with an emphasis in music composition.



MILITARY SERVICE

Adam enlisted in the United States Army in 2016.
He attended Basic Combat Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri.
Upon completion of his Basic Training, Adam went on to Fort Huachuca, Arizona,
where he learned the skillsets of a 35T-Military Intelligence Systems Maintainer/Integrator.
He then traveled to Eglin Air Force Base, Florida to serve with the 7th Special Forces Group (Airborne) for three years.
In 2020, Adam separated from Active Duty and became a veteran.



PROFESSIONAL LIFE

After separating from the Army, Adam pursued a career in Systems Administration, eventually leading him to Penn State University.
He is now gainfully employed as a Systems Administration Contractor with TEKSystems for Penn State University.
Adam has five years of Systems Administration experience: four years in the Army, and one as a contractor at Penn State.
